Who We Are

NHS Math is built by dedicated NHS seniors to provide freshmen with easy-to-use Algebra I resources. Our goal is to make learning algebra approachable, organized, and highly effective.

Our Approach

We focus on crystal-clear explanations of algebraic terms, breaking down complex multi-step equations, and providing interactive examples to build confidence block by block.

What we do

NHS Math provides a structured Algebra I repository for freshmen to study units, definitions, and terms efficiently. Resources include explanations, examples, and interactive exercises to strengthen understanding.

Unit Guides

Detailed guides for each Algebra I unit, including key concepts and examples.

Practice Problems

Step-by-step problems for students to apply what they learn.

Concept Explanations

Clear explanations of Algebra I terms and concepts to build foundational knowledge.

Vocabulary Lists

Curated masterlists of definitions, properties, and core mathematical formulas.
